What it does

Bring your chosen AI into the page you are already reading.

TabMate keeps a small assistant button in the corner of normal web pages. Click it and your selected AI opens in a fresh popup instead of pulling you into another full tab.

Why it feels better

  • No extra tab hunt every time you want to ask something.
  • No manual copy-paste of the page title or link.
  • Selection actions carry text into the chat with context attached.

How it works

Set it once. Use it everywhere.

1

Choose ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini.

Pick the assistant you already use most. TabMate remembers it until you change it.

2

Click the corner button from any tab.

It opens a fresh popup chat without taking over the page you were using.

3

Highlight text for quick help.

Explain, Ask, and Simplify send the selected text with the page title and link already attached.
